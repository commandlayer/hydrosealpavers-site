Our Residential Paver Sealing Warranty

3-Year Workmanship & Sealer Performance Warranty

All residential paver sealing services performed by HydroSeal Pavers include a three (3) year warranty from the date of completion.

This warranty covers:

  • Peeling
  • Flaking
  • Abnormal sealer failure due to improper application or workmanship

If a covered issue occurs:

  1. We will inspect the affected area at no cost.
  2. If the issue is confirmed as a covered workmanship/application failure, we will re-seal the affected area one time at no labor charge.

We seal it right — or we make it right.

What This Warranty Does Not Cover

To keep the warranty fair and sustainable, the following are excluded:

  • Normal wear and gradual color fading over time
  • Damage from vehicle traffic, heavy equipment, or improper use
  • Standing water, drainage problems, base failure, or paver movement/settling
  • Efflorescence, moisture vapor issues, or substrate conditions outside our control
  • Chemical exposure (pool chemicals, acids, fertilizers, cleaners)
  • Acts of nature (storms, flooding, hurricanes, extreme weather)
  • Work performed by others after our service

This warranty applies to sealer performance only. It does not warrant the structural integrity of the pavers or the underlying base.
