Important Warranty Terms

  • Coverage period: 2 years starting on the job completion date shown on your invoice/service record.
  • Remedy is limited to inspection and repair/re-seal of covered areas. No cash refunds.
  • Coverage applies to the treated surface only and does not include adjoining areas not serviced.
  • Warranty applies to coating adhesion only. It does not warrant the structural integrity of the pavers or the underlying base.
  • If matching is required, color/gloss match is not guaranteed due to natural variation, wear, and lighting.
  • Owner maintenance requirement: Coverage assumes normal residential use and no pressure washing or harsh chemical cleaning on sealed surfaces after service. If you’re unsure what’s safe, call us first.
  • Transferability: Warranty is tied to the serviced property address and is transferable once within the coverage period with written notice (simple email/receipt update).

What This Warranty Does Not Cover

To keep the warranty fair and sustainable, the following are excluded:

  • Normal wear, tire marks, scuffs, and gradual color fading over time
  • Damage from vehicle traffic, heavy equipment, furniture dragging, or improper use
  • Standing water, drainage problems, base failure, or paver movement/settling
  • Efflorescence, moisture vapor issues, or substrate conditions outside our control
  • Chemical exposure (pool chemicals, acids, fertilizers, degreasers, harsh cleaners)
  • Pressure washing of sealed surfaces or aggressive cleaning methods after service
  • Acts of nature (storms, flooding, hurricanes, extreme weather)
  • Work performed by others after our service, or alterations to the surface

Tip: To protect your sealed surface, avoid harsh chemicals and degreasers and do not pressure wash sealed pavers. If you need cleaning, call us and we’ll recommend a safe method.
