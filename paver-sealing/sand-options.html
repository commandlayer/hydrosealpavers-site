Joint Sand Color Options

The right joint sand color does more than complete the look—it sharpens your paver pattern and supports long-term surface stability. At HydroSeal, we install clean kiln-dried joint sand and lock it in with our sealing system so your restoration performs as one complete process.

Why HydroSeal treats joints as a priority

Joint sand is a structural part of your paver system. When joints wash out, pavers can shift, edges soften, and the surface ages faster. Our restoration process rebuilds and stabilizes the joints before sealing so the finish looks crisp and stays consistent.

  • Cleaner, sharper finished appearance
  • Less sand washout
  • Reduced weeds + insect intrusion
  • More stable segmental system

How to choose with confidence

We recommend selecting your sand color based on both your paver tone and the level of contrast you want after sealing:

  • Tan / Fieldstone: warm tones, hides dust, classic look
  • Gray / Platinum: modern look, matches cool-toned pavers
  • Black: bold contrast, sharp borders (shows debris more)
  • Pearl: light, clean look for pale stone
Not sure? Send a photo. We’ll recommend the best match.
