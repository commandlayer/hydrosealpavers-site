Why Professional Paver Sealing Matters

Joint Sand Stability

Florida rain events can wash out weak joints quickly. Proper re-sanding and stabilization reduce movement, help limit weeds and ant tunneling, and protect the edges of each paver over time.

Easier Maintenance & Stain Resistance

A properly sealed surface is easier to maintain when dealing with organic staining, leaf tannins, and everyday spills. It does not make pavers stain-proof, but it improves cleanup and helps slow deep absorption.

Better Color, Finish, and Long-Term Appearance

UV, humidity, and heavy summer storms wear down untreated pavers fast. Professional prep and controlled sealer application keep color more consistent and help preserve curb appeal throughout Florida seasons.

What We Actually Do on Residential Projects

1) Surface Evaluation

We inspect paver condition, joint integrity, drainage patterns, staining, and any prior sealer issues before finalizing scope and product strategy.

2) Deep Cleaning & Joint Prep

We remove embedded dirt, organics, and failing material so the surface is clean and ready. Prep quality drives final finish quality and long-term performance.

3) Re-Sanding with ASTM-C144 Trident Sand

We re-sand joints to restore structure and improve stability, using Trident ASTM-C144 joint sand selected for the surface and usage demands.

4) Controlled Sealer Application

We apply sealer in controlled passes for even coverage and consistent finish. We avoid rushed application that causes haze, puddling, or uneven sheen.
