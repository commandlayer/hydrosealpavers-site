Why Annual Maintenance Matters

Routine maintenance is critical for the longevity of your sealer and the pavers it protects. Organic buildup, environmental staining, and natural joint movement can gradually impact both appearance and performance. Our controlled maintenance approach keeps surfaces clean, joints stable, and your investment protected — without aggressive washing methods that compromise sealed surfaces.
