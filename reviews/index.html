HydroSeal Care Program

Florida sun, rain, humidity, and traffic wear down exterior surfaces fast. The HydroSeal Care Program is our simple, professional maintenance visit that helps keep sealed pavers clean, bright, and protected — and prevents small issues from turning into expensive resets.

Why Maintenance Matters

Routine maintenance is critical for the longevity of your sealer and the pavers it protects. Mold, mildew, algae, and organic buildup don’t just look bad — over time they can stain surfaces, weaken joints, and create slick areas. We use a controlled process (not aggressive blasting) to keep your exterior looking right year-round.

What’s Included

A professional maintenance visit for previously sealed pavers, built for clean surfaces, stable joints, and consistent appearance. Most visits are scheduled in a simple morning window — in and out without long dry-time downtime.

Plans start at $0.30 per sq ft. Final pricing depends on condition, access, and scope.

Included Steps
  • Professional softwash treatment to safely remove organic growth and surface contaminants
  • Deep surface cleaning pass to lift grime, buildup, and embedded debris
  • Surface brightening treatment for a cleaner, more uniform appearance
  • Final rinse and detail pass for a consistent, finished look
  • Targeted joint sand reinforcement where minor washout is present
  • Comprehensive surface inspection with notes on maintenance and reseal timing
What This Visit Does
  • Keeps sealed surfaces cleaner and brighter between reseals
  • Stops minor joint washout from turning into larger erosion
  • Maintains curb appeal so you don’t wait until it “looks bad”
  • Keeps you in our rotation for easier scheduling
Care Program Member Benefit: Receive a 10% credit toward your next full reseal when you stay in the program and reseal within our recommended window.
