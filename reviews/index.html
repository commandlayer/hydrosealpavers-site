HydroSeal Care Program

Available exclusively for surfaces previously sealed by HydroSeal.

You invested in sealing your pavers correctly. Now protect that investment with structured annual care. Florida sun, moisture, and daily use gradually impact even well-sealed surfaces. The HydroSeal Care Program keeps your pavers clean, your joints stable, and your reseal timing on track — so everything stays consistent and predictable.

This is lifecycle care for a surface that was sealed right.

Why Ongoing Care Matters

Even a properly applied sealer benefits from routine oversight. Without annual care, appearance can vary over time — buildup returns, joints may loosen in spots, and reseal timing becomes harder to judge. With structured annual care, surface performance stays consistent and measurable.

What Your Annual Care Visit Includes

  • Professional softwash treatment designed for sealed pavers
  • Controlled deep-clean pass
  • Surface brightening for consistent appearance
  • Spot joint sand reinforcement where needed
  • Full condition inspection
  • Performance-based reseal timing recommendation
Built for maintained, HydroSeal-sealed surfaces. If restoration is needed, recommendations are provided before any work begins.

Lifecycle Alignment

Consistent maintenance supports long-term sealer performance and helps identify the proper reseal window based on surface condition — not guesswork.

The Care Program is maintenance-focused and designed to complement the HydroSeal system over its full service life.

Who This Program Is For

Available exclusively for surfaces previously sealed by HydroSeal. This program continues the standards and maintenance philosophy established during the original sealing process.

Warranty Relationship

Our 2-Year Warranty covers workmanship and sealer adhesion. The Care Program focuses on appearance consistency, joint stability, and performance monitoring between reseals. It is a structured maintenance approach — not a warranty extension.
