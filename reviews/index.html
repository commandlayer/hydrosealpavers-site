What’s Included

Annual Protection Visit (Residential)

A professional maintenance visit for previously sealed pavers, built for clean surfaces, stable joints, and consistent appearance. Most visits are scheduled in a simple morning window — in and out without long dry-time downtime.

Included Steps
  • Controlled bleach softwash treatment (professional dilution and application)
  • Surface cleaning pass to lift organics, grime, and buildup
  • Brightening pass (wipeout/whitener) for a cleaner, more even look
  • Final rinse/clean pass for consistent finish
  • Spot polymeric sand reinforcement where joints have minor washout
  • Surface health inspection + notes on reseal timing
What This Visit Does
  • Helps keep sealed surfaces cleaner and brighter between reseals
  • Stops minor joint washout from turning into larger erosion
  • Maintains appearance so you don’t wait until it “looks bad”
  • Keeps you in our rotation for easier scheduling
Maintain your investment at roughly 75% less than a full reseal.

Pricing

Annual Protection Visits are priced at $0.35 per sq ft. A minimum may apply based on travel and surface conditions. We’ll confirm total price after a quick look at the area and scope.

Important Notes
  • This is a maintenance visit — it is not a full reseal and does not include a flood coat or full sealer application.
  • We use a controlled process and avoid aggressive pressure washing that can damage sealed surfaces.
  • If you want a true “refresh coat” (sealer reinforcement), we can quote that separately depending on surface condition and wear.

Who It’s For

  • Homeowners who want their pavers to stay clean and sharp year-round
  • Clients within 6–24 months of a sealing service (or surfaces in good condition)
  • Driveways, patios, pool decks, walkways, and porches with stable pavers
