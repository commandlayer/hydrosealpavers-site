Florida Surfaces Need Ongoing Care

Sun, rain, humidity, and daily traffic gradually impact sealed pavers. Even a properly applied sealer benefits from routine maintenance to keep surfaces looking consistent and joints performing as intended.

  • Remove organic buildup and environmental staining
  • Prevent minor joint washout before it spreads
  • Maintain color consistency and curb appeal
  • Catch small issues early — before they become expensive

Your Annual Care Visit Includes

  • Seal-safe softwash treatment
  • Controlled deep cleaning pass
  • Surface brightening for uniform appearance
  • Spot joint sand reinforcement (where needed)
  • Full surface inspection
  • Reseal timing recommendation
This is maintenance — not restoration. It preserves good surfaces and keeps them on track.
