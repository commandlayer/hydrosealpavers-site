Warranty & expectations

What we warranty

We’ll provide clear warranty terms in the proposal (based on surface type, product selection, and usage). If there’s an issue tied to our application, we handle it.

  • Even application standards (no obvious blotchy sections)
  • Clear cure/dry time guidance and traffic rules
  • Punch list + post-job walkthrough

What affects lifespan

Florida sun, traffic, irrigation overspray, and existing surface condition all matter. We’ll recommend the right finish and a maintenance plan that makes sense for your budget.

  • Traffic volume (amenities vs. low-traffic walkways)
  • Sprinklers / standing water zones
  • Old coatings or previous sealer failures
  • Stain risk (trash areas, grills, parking drips)
