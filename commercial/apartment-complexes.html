Our process (designed for occupied properties)

Apartment work fails when crews block access, miss communication, or leave inconsistent results. We run a clean plan that management can rely on.

  1. Scope mapping: zones, high-traffic areas, amenity priorities
  2. Cleaning: correct chemistry + rinse (not harsh damage)
  3. Joint sand: stabilize joints where applicable
  4. Sealing: even application, clean edges, controlled finish
  5. QA walkthrough: punch list + final check with management
Resident messaging support
We can provide simple notice language: dates, affected areas, parking guidance, and cure time expectations.

Warranty & expectations

What you can expect

  • Clear scope with inclusions/exclusions
  • Phased schedule to keep access open
  • Uniform finish standards across zones
  • Clean edges and controlled application
  • Final walkthrough + punch list closeout

What affects lifespan

  • Foot traffic volume (amenity vs. low-traffic zones)
  • Sprinklers / standing water areas
  • Old coatings or prior failures
  • Stain risk zones (trash, grills, parking drips)
  • Finish choice (natural vs. enhanced)

Warranty terms

Warranty language is included in the proposal and is based on surface type, product selection, and usage. If there’s an issue tied to our application, we handle it.
