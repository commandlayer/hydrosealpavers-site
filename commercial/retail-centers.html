Our process (built for commercial properties)

1) Walkthrough + scope

We identify high-visibility zones, stain areas, shade/algae risk, and any slip-sensitive surfaces. You get a clear scope (by zone) so approvals are simple.

2) Schedule that doesn’t break operations

We plan work windows around tenants and foot traffic. Most retail centers prefer early morning or night work. We can phase by building, by courtyard, or by priority zones.

3) Clean + detail

Proper chemistry and controlled cleaning. Edges matter in retail: storefront lines, curbs, and entrances get detail work.

4) Seal / protect (when applicable)

For pavers: re-sand / stabilize (as needed), then apply the right sealer for your site’s finish goals. Optional anti-slip additive for slick zones.

Stormwater & safety (why this matters)

Commercial properties can’t afford “wash water down the drain” behavior. We treat site drainage seriously: we plan work zones, protect storm drains, and reduce debris before washing. We also operate with PPE and safe work practices.

Storm-drain aware practices
  • Dry pickup first (blow/sweep) to reduce runoff debris
  • Block/protect drains in active work zones
  • Controlled rinse to keep the site clean and compliant
Safety + professionalism
  • Cones / signage / controlled work zones
  • PPE appropriate to the task
  • Clear communication with on-site contacts
