Builder / new construction FAQ

Can you do multiple homes per day?

Yes — that’s why contract packaging exists. With consistent scopes we can batch work efficiently.

Do you handle efflorescence?

We can treat efflorescence where appropriate. We’ll evaluate severity and surface type first.

Do you use high pressure on siding?

No. Soft wash for siding and roofs is the safe standard. Controlled pressure is used only when appropriate.

Can sealing be offered as a buyer upgrade?

Yes — we can price it as a per-home upgrade option, and you decide how to bundle it into your contracts.
