Eagle Landing Paver Sealing Questions

How long should we wait before driving on a sealed driveway?

Driveways usually need a longer cure window than patios or walkways.

We typically recommend 24–48 hours before vehicle traffic based on weather conditions.

Do active family areas need more frequent resealing?

Often, yes. High-use patios and pool decks can wear faster than low-traffic areas.

We evaluate traffic patterns and recommend a practical maintenance timeline.

Can you handle driveway and pool deck sealing in the same visit?

Yes. We stage work so each area gets proper prep and cure time.

This keeps the final result consistent across connected outdoor spaces.

Will sealing reduce cleanup from cookouts and pool activity?

Yes. Sealed pavers absorb spills and grime more slowly, making cleanup easier.

It does not eliminate maintenance, but it improves day-to-day care.

Is joint sand really necessary if pavers still look stable?

Yes. Joint sand can degrade before movement becomes obvious.

Resetting and compacting sand helps preserve lockup and edge performance.

What if parts of our deck get more sun than others?

Mixed exposure is common in Eagle Landing.

We adjust timing and application to keep cure and finish as uniform as possible.
