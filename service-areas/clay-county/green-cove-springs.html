Green Cove Springs Paver Sealing Questions

Can badly weathered pavers in Green Cove Springs still be sealed?

Often, yes—after proper restoration.

We deep clean, correct joints, and verify dry conditions before any sealer is applied.

Is sealing a realistic alternative to replacing older pavers?

In many situations it is.

If the paver field is structurally sound, restoration plus sealing can deliver a major visual and functional improvement.

How do you handle organic buildup from humid conditions?

We use targeted cleaning methods to remove algae, mildew, and embedded grime before sealing.

That prep step is critical for long-term performance.

Will joint sand wash out quickly after heavy rain?

Not when joints are properly re-sanded, compacted, and sealed.

This combination helps improve lockup and resistance to washout.

How soon can patios be used again after sealing?

Light foot traffic is often possible the same day under good weather.

For normal use, we usually recommend waiting about 24 hours.

How frequently should Green Cove Springs pavers be resealed?

Typical timing is around every 2–3 years.

More shade, moisture, or heavy traffic may require a shorter interval.
