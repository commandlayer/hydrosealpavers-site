Why Paver Sealing Is Worth It (When It’s Done Correctly)

The goal isn’t “shine.” The goal is protection, stable joints, and easier maintenance — with a finish that looks clean, not overdone.

Locks In Joint Sand

In Northeast Florida, joint sand failure is one of the most common reasons pavers shift, settle, and deteriorate over time. After joints are fully cleaned out, we install professional-grade ASTM-C144 Trident joint sand and compact it correctly so it locks in place.

ASTM-C144 Joint Stability
Reduced Washout In Heavy Rain
Helps Prevent Weeds & Ants
Tighter, Longer-Lasting Joints
What most companies skip: joint prep + correct sand depth + proper compaction.

Helps Resist Stains

Professional paver sealing helps slow absorption from oil, dirt, and organic staining. No sealer is stain-proof, but a quality sealer gives you time to clean spills before they penetrate.
