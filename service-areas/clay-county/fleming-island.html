Fleming Island Paver Sealing Questions

Can older pavers in Fleming Island usually be restored instead of replaced?

In many cases, yes. If the base and layout are still sound, cleaning, joint repair, and sealing can significantly improve appearance and performance.

We assess condition first and recommend replacement only when it is truly necessary.

Does heavy shade change how sealing should be done?

Yes. Shade slows drying and can increase mildew pressure.

We plan around moisture levels and weather windows so sealer cures properly.

How does sealing help with pool deck maintenance?

Sealing reduces absorption and helps limit deep staining from splash zones and organic debris.

It also makes routine cleaning more manageable over time.

Will re-sanding make a visible difference on established pavers?

Absolutely. Properly compacted joint sand sharpens joint lines and improves structural lockup.

It is a key step in both performance and finished appearance.

How often should mature hardscapes be resealed?

Most Fleming Island homes benefit from a 2–3 year reseal cycle.

Timing depends on sun exposure, traffic, and how quickly finish wear appears.

Can you seal driveways, patios, and pool decks during one project?

Yes. We frequently service complete property hardscapes in a single phased plan.

That keeps finish quality and timeline consistent.
