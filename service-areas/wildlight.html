Our Wildlight paver sealing process

We begin by evaluating the condition of the pavers and identifying what the surface needs before sealing. That includes cleaning, checking joint sand, and preparing the surface so sealer is not being applied over avoidable problems. When joints need fresh sand, we re-sand before sealing. Once prep is complete, we apply premium sealer with attention to consistency, finish quality, and the overall look of the area.

Driveways, patios, pool decks, and walkways. Different paver areas age in different ways. Driveways often show traffic wear, fading, and staining first. Patios and walkways can start to look dry or washed out. Pool deck areas may need special attention to surface condition and finish consistency. We adjust the prep and sealing approach based on where the pavers are located and what kind of wear they have taken on.

How much does paver sealing cost in Wildlight?

Most jobs start at $1.50 per square foot. Final pricing depends on square footage, prep requirements, joint condition, and overall surface wear.

Do you seal pool decks and patios?

Yes. We seal driveways, patios, pool decks, walkways, and similar residential paver surfaces.

Do pavers need to be re-sanded before sealing?

Sometimes. If the joints have lost sand or need stabilization, re-sanding is an important part of the process before sealing.

Can sealing improve the color of older pavers?

In many cases it can improve depth of color and create a more finished look, though results depend on the existing condition of the surface.

How long does the process take?

Many jobs can be completed in one day under the right conditions, but some projects need additional time depending on weather, moisture, and prep needs.

How do I know my pavers are ready to be sealed again?

Common signs include fading, a dull surface, noticeable sand loss, staining, and an overall dry or worn appearance.
