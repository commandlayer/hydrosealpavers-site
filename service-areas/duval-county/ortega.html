Ortega Paver Sealing Questions

How often should pavers be sealed in Ortega?

Most surfaces in Ortega do best on a 2–3 year reseal cycle, depending on exposure. Driveways in full sun wear faster; shaded patios can last longer, but they often need more cleaning because moisture lingers.

Will sealing help with algae/mildew in shaded areas?

It helps by reducing absorption and making routine rinsing easier, but it starts with proper cleaning. Sealing over organic growth can trap discoloration, so we clean correctly and allow true dry-down before sealing.

Can you keep walking areas from getting too slick?

Yes. Slickness usually comes from over-application or the wrong finish in walking zones. We apply controlled coats and can recommend traction options where it makes sense (especially around pools).
