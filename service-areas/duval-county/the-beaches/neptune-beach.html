Why Beach-Adjacent Pavers Need Sealing

UV, humidity, rain, and organic growth all work against unprotected pavers. Add regular foot traffic and water flow, and joint erosion becomes a common issue. Sealing after proper prep helps preserve the look of the surface while improving long-term cleanup and maintenance.

HydroSeal Prep and Sealing Process

  1. Inspect condition, joints, and prior coatings.
  2. Deep clean to remove buildup and biological growth.
  3. Re-sand joints where support has dropped.
  4. Apply premium sealer in even, controlled passes.

Neptune Beach Paver Sealing FAQ

What causes pavers in Neptune Beach to look dark or faded?

Moisture, shade, mildew, and sun exposure can all contribute to dark or faded appearance over time.

Can joint sand loss lead to weeds and movement?

Yes. Low joints make movement, weed growth, and ant tunneling more likely if not restored and stabilized.

Do you handle both driveways and patios in Neptune Beach?

Yes. We handle driveway, patio, walkway, and pool deck surfaces using the same prep-first process.
