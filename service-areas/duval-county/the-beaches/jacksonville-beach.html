Why Jacksonville Beach Pavers Need Coastal Protection

UV exposure, humidity, storm rain, organic growth, and daily use all wear on paver systems. Over time that can mean patchy color, grime retention, and low joints. Sealing after proper prep helps slow that wear and keeps surfaces easier to manage.

Jacksonville Beach Paver Sealing FAQ

Is paver sealing worth it for pool decks and driveways in Jacksonville Beach?

Yes for most homeowners. Sealing helps preserve appearance and makes high-use surfaces easier to clean and maintain.

What is included in HydroSeal’s prep process before sealing?

How often should Jacksonville Beach pavers be resealed?

Most pavers are resealed every 2 to 3 years, depending on exposure, traffic, and how quickly the current finish wears.
