Our Yulee paver sealing process

Every project starts with surface evaluation and prep. We clean the pavers, address buildup and problem areas, and check the condition of the joints before moving forward. If the surface needs fresh sand, we re-sand with the appropriate joint sand before sealing. Once the surface is ready, we apply premium sealer with a focus on even coverage, clean finish quality, and the right look for the space.

How much does paver sealing cost in Yulee?

Most jobs start at $1.50 per square foot, with final pricing based on condition, prep requirements, joint sand needs, and total surface area.

Can my sealing job be completed in one day?

Many projects can be completed in one day during favorable conditions, especially in warmer months. Some jobs may need additional time depending on moisture, weather, surface condition, or the amount of prep required.

Do you re-sand pavers before sealing?

Yes, when the joints need it. Proper joint sand condition is important to the finished result and long-term performance of the surface.

What areas do you seal?

We seal driveways, patios, pool decks, walkways, and other residential paver surfaces.

Will sealing make faded pavers look better?

In many cases, yes. Sealing can help restore richer color and create a more finished appearance, though results still depend on the age and condition of the pavers.

How do I know if my pavers need sealing?

Common signs include fading, dull color, loose joints, sand loss, staining, and a dry or uneven surface appearance.
