Beachwalk Paver Sealing Questions

Do pavers in Beachwalk need extra maintenance because of the lagoon environment?

Often, yes. Higher moisture and humidity can increase organic buildup and surface wear, so proactive cleaning and sealing are important.

Can sealing help with grime and buildup on Beachwalk pavers?

Yes. Sealed pavers are less absorbent, which helps reduce deep-set grime and makes regular cleaning more effective.

Will sealing help keep my Beachwalk patio cleaner between services?

It typically does. Sealing creates a protective layer that helps dirt and spills release more easily during routine maintenance.

Do you re-sand joints before sealing in Beachwalk?

Yes. We remove weak joint material and install kiln-dried sand before sealing to improve lockup and reduce washout risk.

What is the cure time after paver sealing in Beachwalk?

Most surfaces are dry to the touch in a few hours. We generally recommend 24 hours before foot traffic and 24–48 hours before vehicles, based on weather.

Is paver sealing mainly about appearance or protection?

Both. Sealing improves color and finish while also helping defend against moisture, staining, and premature joint deterioration.
