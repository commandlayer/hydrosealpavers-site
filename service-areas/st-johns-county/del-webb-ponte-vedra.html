Our Professional 6 Step Process

Del Webb Ponte Vedra surfaces often need more than a surface wash and quick topcoat. Our process is built for established driveways, patios, walkways, and poolside hardscapes that need careful prep before sealing.

  1. Inspection: review wear patterns, shade exposure, drainage, joint condition, and overall surface age
  2. Clean: use controlled cleaning and targeted treatment to remove buildup, grime, and organic staining
  3. Re-sand: install and compact kiln-dried joint sand where joints have weakened or washed out; available sand colors can be recommended when appropriate
  4. Dry & verify: confirm moisture levels and weather conditions before sealing
  5. Seal: apply even coats with controlled finish level and clean transitions
  6. Final walkthrough: review cure time, care guidance, and long-term maintenance recommendations

Del Webb Ponte Vedra Paver Sealing Questions

Is paver sealing worth it for Del Webb Ponte Vedra homeowners?

Yes. Properly sealed pavers generally stay cleaner, look more consistent, and require less intensive upkeep over time.

Can aging pavers usually be restored instead of replaced?

In many cases, yes. If the installation is structurally sound, cleaning, re-sanding, and sealing can restore appearance without full replacement.

Do you re-sand joints before sealing in Del Webb Ponte Vedra?

Yes. We replace and compact weak or missing joint sand before sealing so the surface performs better.

Will sealing help reduce mildew, washout, and uneven color?

It can. Sealing helps reduce absorption and supports a more even finish while making mildew and grime easier to manage.

How long should we stay off the surface after sealing?

We usually recommend about 24 hours before regular foot traffic and up to 48 hours before vehicles, depending on conditions.

How often should pavers in Del Webb Ponte Vedra be resealed?

Most properties benefit from resealing about every 2–3 years, based on exposure, traffic, and weathering. For scheduled upkeep between reseals, ask about our care program.
