How to maintain sealed pavers

The fastest way to ruin a fresh seal is aggressive cleaning or harsh chemicals. Follow this and your surface stays cleaner longer.

Do

  • Rinse periodically to remove dust and debris
  • Use mild soap and a gentle brush for small spots
  • Address spills quickly (especially oil)
  • Call us if you’re unsure how to clean a stain

Don’t

  • Pressure wash sealed pavers aggressively
  • Use acids, strong degreasers, or harsh cleaners
  • Drag heavy furniture across the surface
  • Assume “more product” is better — over-application causes problems
Want the easiest maintenance plan? Ask about periodic cleaning and touch-up scheduling for high-traffic areas.

What affects the price of paver sealing

Square footage matters — but so do prep needs. Two driveways can be the same size and require completely different work.

Condition & staining

Heavier organic growth, rust, oil, and deep stains require additional treatment and time.

Joint condition

Washed-out joints or missing sand require proper repair and stabilization to avoid shifting and weeds.

Surface type & environment

Pool decks, shaded areas, and high-moisture conditions often require a different approach and extra dry-time planning.
