What sealing should do for your Nocatee pavers

A premium job isn’t about “making it shiny.” It’s about stability, uniformity, and making the surface easier to keep clean — without creating tacky spots or blotches. Here’s what you should expect when it’s done correctly.

Richer, more even finish

Uniform coverage with clean edges — no puddling, no striping, no “wet look” overload unless you want it.

Stronger joints

Re-sanded joints reduce washout, weeds, and ant tunneling. The surface feels tighter underfoot (and under tires).

Easier maintenance

Quality sealer helps reduce absorption from grime and organics so routine rinsing and spot cleaning is easier.

Reality check: sealing doesn’t fix base issues. If pavers are sinking, rocking, or holding water, we’ll tell you what needs correction first.

Common Nocatee paver issues (and what we do about them)

If you’re seeing any of these, it’s usually a sign that joint sand needs attention, organics are building up, or an old coating is failing. We’ll be straightforward about what’s cleanable, what’s permanent, and what requires a different fix.

Sand washout + weeds

We clean correctly, restore joint depth, and stabilize the joints so storms don’t undo the work in a month.

Organic staining

We treat growth and grime before sealing. Sealing over organics traps it and makes the finish look blotchy.

Blotchy / uneven finish

Usually from over-application or moisture. Our approach is controlled passes with a plan for edges and overlap.

Nocatee paver sealing FAQ (32081)

Straight answers so you know what you’re buying.

How long does a paver sealing job take?

Most residential surfaces are completed in a day, but dry-time and cure guidance depends on weather and surface condition. If conditions aren’t right, we wait — rushing is how sealers fail.

Can you fix stains?

Many organic stains and surface grime improve significantly with correct cleaning. Some stains are permanent depending on the source and time. We’ll tell you what’s realistic before we start.

Will sealing stop weeds forever?

It helps by stabilizing joints and reducing washout, but nothing makes pavers “weed-proof.” The goal is fewer weeds and easier maintenance.
