Why Nocatee surfaces fail early

Paver sealing in Nocatee is a prep game.

Nocatee pavers deal with intense UV, sudden storms, and humidity swings. That combo exposes shortcuts fast: streaking, white haze, sand washout, and premature failure. A premium result comes from correct cleaning, joint restoration, dry-time discipline, and controlled sealing — in that order.

  • Deep clean that removes organics without destroying joints
  • Re-sand joints to correct depth and compact properly
  • Dry-time discipline so the sealer bonds and cures correctly
  • Even coats with clean edges (no puddling, no over-application)
