Is pool deck sealing worth it in Shearwater?

Yes. Pool decks see constant moisture, sunscreen, and foot traffic. Sealing helps protect appearance, limit deep staining, and keep maintenance manageable.

Will sealing help with color loss on Shearwater pavers?

It can. Professional sealing enhances existing color and slows visible fading caused by sun exposure and weathering.

Do you handle driveways and pool decks differently?

Yes. We adjust cleaning approach, prep detail, and sealer application to match each surface’s use, exposure, and traffic level.

Will sealed pavers be easier to keep clean after entertaining?

Generally, yes. Sealed surfaces are less absorbent, so everyday grime and spills are easier to remove during normal maintenance.

How long is cure time after paver sealing in Shearwater?

Most surfaces are touch-dry in a few hours, with about 24 hours recommended before foot traffic and 24–48 hours before vehicles, depending on conditions.

Do you re-sand before sealing in Shearwater?

Always when needed. Proper joint re-sanding and compaction are key parts of long-term paver performance and should happen before sealer is applied.
