Why Florida Surfaces Require Ongoing Care

Even a properly applied sealer benefits from routine maintenance to preserve uniform appearance and joint stability. Annual care keeps surfaces looking consistent and helps prevent small issues from becoming restoration-level problems.

Annual care helps
  • Remove organic buildup and environmental staining
  • Prevent minor joint washout before it spreads
  • Maintain color consistency and curb appeal
  • Catch small issues early, when they’re easy to correct
Important scope note

This program protects well-maintained surfaces. It is not designed to rescue neglected surfaces or failing sealers.

What Your Annual Care Visit Includes

  • Softwash treatment
  • Deep Clean
  • Surface brightening for uniform appearance
  • Spot joint sand reinforcement (where needed)
  • Full surface inspection
  • Condition-based reseal timing recommendation
Each visit follows the same standards established during your original HydroSeal service.

Earn Up to 20% Toward Your Next Reseal

The Care Program rewards consistency.

Up to 20% total credit may be applied toward your next full reseal when completed within the recommended performance window (typically within 36 months of original service).

Example: $1,700 reseal → 20% credit = $340 applied
