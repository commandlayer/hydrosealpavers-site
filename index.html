What sealing actually does

A premium result comes from proper prep + stabilized joints + an even application. The goal is protection that looks clean — not sticky, blotchy, or over-glossed.

Locks in joint sand

Helps reduce washout, shifting, and weeds taking over the joints.

Helps resist stains

Oil drips, rust, dirt, and organic growth are easier to remove when the surface is protected.

Restores a “finished” look

Color enhancement + uniform finish makes the entire property look sharper and higher-end.

Our process (the part that matters)

Prep makes the finish. We focus on correct chemistry, controlled rinsing, clean edges, and even application so the surface looks premium and lasts.

  1. Inspection: surface condition, old coatings, problem areas
  2. Clean: right mix + rinse to lift organics, grime, and discoloration
  3. Re-sand: refill joints for stabilization (polymeric when appropriate)
  4. Seal: even coat, consistent finish, tight edge control
  5. Walkthrough: care notes + dry time and traffic guidance
