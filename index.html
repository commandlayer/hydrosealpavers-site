What makes it premium (and why it lasts)

We build the job like a system: restore the surface, lock the joints, then seal with a professional-grade Trident finish. Trident Hurricane CAT 5 is a 2-part urethane sealer designed for deep enhancement and joint stabilization (hardening the sand to reduce washout, weeds, and insect intrusion). It also includes antifungal film protection.

Step-by-step (how we prevent blotchy results + early failure)
  1. Inspection: surface condition, prior coatings, oil/rust hotspots, drainage issues, and joint depth.
  2. Deep Clean + Correct Chemistry: we lift organics and embedded grime so the sealer bonds to the surface (not to dirt).
  3. Re-sand the joints (Cat-5): we replace washed-out joint sand with clean, kiln-dried sand for stability and a crisp finished look.
  4. Seal with Trident Hurricane CAT 5: controlled coverage, clean edges, no puddling, no overspray mess. Joint stabilization is built into the system.
  5. Final Walkthrough: cure time, return-to-service guidance, and simple care rules so it stays sharp.
Pro tip: The driveway look comes from even prep + full joints. Most failures happen because the joints were ignored.

What driveway sealing actually does

A proper driveway seal restores color, helps stabilize joints, and adds a protective barrier against stains and weathering. It also makes routine maintenance easier—less grime sticks, and cleanup is faster.

  • Color enhancement + uniform “finished” appearance
  • Joint stabilization (less washout + fewer weeds)
  • Stain resistance (oil/rust/dirt clean easier)
  • Surface protection against sun + rain wear
